# Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how {{PLATFORM_NAME}} (the "Platform") collects, uses, and shares personal information. It applies to all users of the Platform, including organization administrators, members, students, and buyers.

## 2. Information We Collect

- Account information: name and email address when you sign in with Google or a magic link.
- Organization information: organization names, membership, and campaign details created by users.
- Order information: items purchased, shipping name and address, and email address for order updates.
- Uploaded content: design files and images uploaded for campaigns.
- Feedback: messages submitted through the in-app feedback form.

Payment card details are collected and processed by Stripe. The Platform never sees or stores your full card number.

## 3. How We Use Information

- To operate campaigns, process orders, and deliver merchandise.
- To send transactional emails such as sign-in links, order confirmations, and shipping notifications.
- To pay campaign proceeds to organizations through Stripe Connect.
- To maintain the security of the Platform and prevent abuse.

We do not sell personal information.

## 4. Service Providers

The Platform shares information with service providers only as needed to operate:

- Stripe — payment processing and organization payouts
- Printful — printing and shipping merchandise (receives recipient name and shipping address)
- Resend — transactional email delivery
- Google — optional sign-in with Google
- Turso — database hosting
- Cloudflare R2 — storage of uploaded design files
- Vercel — application hosting
- OpenAI — optional AI design generation (receives design prompts only)

## 5. Data Retention

Account and order records are retained while your account is active and as required for tax, accounting, and legal purposes. You may request deletion of your account data by contacting {{SUPPORT_EMAIL}}.

## 6. Security

## 7. Children

The Platform is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. Student participation is expected to occur under organization and parental supervision.
